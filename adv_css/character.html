Yellowjackets: Characters Shauna Shipman Played by: Sophie Nélisse (Teen), Melanie Lynskey (Adult)

Then: A quiet but sharp-witted member of the Yellowjackets, Shauna harbors secrets, including a betrayal that complicates her closest friendships. Now: As an adult, Shauna (played by Melanie Lynskey) struggles with suburban dissatisfaction while hiding dark impulses from her past.

Natalie “Nat” Scatorccio Played by: Sophie Thatcher (Teen), Juliette Lewis (Adult)

Then: A rebellious, tough girl with a complicated home life, Nat finds herself relying on survival skills that make her a key player in the wilderness. Now: Adult Nat (Juliette Lewis) battles addiction and demons from the past while trying to uncover the truth behind the group’s history.
