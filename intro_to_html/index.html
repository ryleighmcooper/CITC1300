Yellowjackets

Yellowjackets is a thriller about a high school girls' soccer team whose plane crashes in the wilderness, leaving them stranded. The series alternates between their struggle for survival in the 1990s and their lives as adults, haunted by dark secrets from their time in the wild. It explores themes of trauma, survival, and the psychological impact of their experiences.

Characters

The Yellowjackets characters include Shauna Sadecki, often seen as the group's leader; Jackie Taylor, the "Queen Bee"; Natalie "Nat" Scatorccio, whose rebellious nature and tough persona earn her the nickname "Nat"; Misty Quigley, sometimes called "Misty the Freak" for her quirky ways; Tawny "Taissa" Turner, who is known for her ambition and drive; and Lottie Matthews, who is often just referred to as "Lottie" and becomes a spiritual figure in the group. As adults, Shauna remains the quiet leader, Natalie still goes by "Nat," Misty stays eccentric, Taissa is focused on surviving at all costs, and Lottie retains her mysterious presence, with each character haunted by the trauma of their past.
