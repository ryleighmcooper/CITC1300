Stranger Things: Characters

Eleven

Millie Bobby Brown

A quiet but powerful girl with psychokinetic abilities who escaped from a secret lab and becomes the heart of the group.

Mike Wheeler

Finn Wolfhard

Smart, loyal, and the emotional core of the group. Mike is one of Eleven’s closest friends and later her boyfriend.

Will Byers

Noah Schnapp

Sensitive and artistic, Will’s disappearance in Season 1 sets everything in motion. He shares a strange connection with the Upside Down.

Lucas Sinclair

Caleb McLaughlin

Level-headed and brave, Lucas is skeptical at times but always stands up for his friends.

Dustin Henderson

Gaten Matarazzo

Witty and science-loving, Dustin is always ready with a plan—and a sense of humor.
